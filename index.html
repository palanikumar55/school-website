About the school

Rooted in Vellanguli, built for every student who walks in

Government Higher Secondary School, Vellanguli serves students from Ambasamudram block and the surrounding villages of Tirunelveli district, offering free, quality education from Class 6 through Class 12 under the Tamil Nadu School Education Department.

What sets this school apart is consistency — a 100% pass result year after year, achieved not by chance but by a faculty that knows every student by name and infrastructure that's maintained, not just installed.

The school follows the Tamil Nadu State Board syllabus, with focused remedial classes for slow learners and extra coaching in the run-up to board examinations, which is a large part of how the 100% record has held.

Infrastructure

Facilities that are actually usable, not just built

Every facility below is maintained and in active use — not a one-time inauguration that's since gone quiet.

📚

Library

A well-stocked reading room with textbooks, reference material, and competitive exam guides available to every student.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs equipped for the practical component of the board syllabus.

💻

Computer Lab

A functioning computer lab supporting the Computer Science stream and basic digital literacy for all classes.

🏫

Smart Classrooms

Digital teaching aids in senior classrooms, used to supplement — not replace — direct classroom teaching.

🏃

Playground & Sports

An open playground for athletics, kabaddi, and volleyball, with annual sports day participation across all classes.

🍽️

Mid-Day Meal Kitchen

A clean, government-scheme kitchen serving nutritious meals daily to support attendance and health.
